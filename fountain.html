Blooming Plant Fountain

A refreshing boulder that helps you mystify (and bloom) deserted spaces.
  • Maker: Nature Club Activists
  • Production cost: Free, it was made from local sculptures & plants from wild & a donated old kitchen robot hand.
  • Tools / Parts used: Plant Bombs & Concrete Rocks, A Robot Hand to squeeze natural flavors into the air.
  • Risks of using this device: Become a wild creeper taking over public spaces. Become habitat of wild insects & birds.
  • Intended to be used by: Humans reinvigorating their sensitivity to nature.
