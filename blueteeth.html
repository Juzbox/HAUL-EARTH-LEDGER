Blueteeth

An in-your-mouth teeth replacement that helps access clean drinking water from a connection to its source (read: heritage) so you respect it more (connect to a source anywhere in the world).
  • Maker: Aditya Prakash
  • Production cost: 50 years.
  • Tools / Parts used: Dropnet, wet metal5.0, niagrastacks, ceramic, wetflix, the deep web, tunnelwhale, cloudburst, and politickle (a device that erases the memories of government workers).
  • Risks of using this device: It's still in the clinical trials phase so will keep you posted about this. Most recent side effect uncovered: occasional brain freeze.
  • Intended to be used by: Those who don't have access to water.
