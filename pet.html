My First Robot

A cute, safe and battery free robot pet that helps kids learn how to take care of their future robot pet.
  • Maker: Dr. Robittle
  • Production cost: 15% of monthly UBI
  • Tools / Parts used: Ikea pot plants, trolley wheels, stuffed animal eyes,Hot Glue, elastic bands.
  • Risks of using this device: Children could get too attached, they can only bring it to wheelchair accessible places.
  • Intended to be used by: Parents who don't want to buy an expensive robot dog but want their kids to be quiet and content.
